Jager: A case study on meta defining power

The metagame is typically formed by singular characters or strategies that are oppressive and prevent other ways of playing the game from being viable. In short, most things that define a meta are completely over powered when compared to their counterparts. However, in Rainbow 6 Seige, Jager, an operator in the game, has an insane play rate and an above average win rate. Despite this he has a relatively low ban rate and is not very commonly compalined about by the community. What is going on here? Can something be meta defining without being overpowered?
